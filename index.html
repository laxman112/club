A Few Words About Our Club



Golfield was founded with the main goal to help all golf players, both professional and amateur, to improve their skills.


Located not far from a forest of eucalyptus and pine trees with the back-drop of the snowcapped Taurus Mountains, our Golf Club has something to offer any player, even with a little experience.

ABOUT


Since the opening of Golfield on August 15, 1995, the Club has become a popular tourist destination attracting golfers. Carved through a forest of eucalyptus and pine trees with the backdrop of the snow-capped Taurus Mountains and an abundance of natural lakes, our club tests all abilities.
