ABOUT


Since the opening of Golfield on August 15, 1995, the Club has become a popular tourist destination attracting golfers. Carved through a forest of eucalyptus and pine trees with the backdrop of the snow-capped Taurus Mountains and an abundance of natural lakes, our club tests all abilities.
